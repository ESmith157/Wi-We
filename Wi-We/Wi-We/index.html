RevPro

RevPro

REVPRO is a comprehensive revenue protection system designed specifically for Utilities and Municipalities. Using the latest mobile technology and GIS the system gives you complete visibility and control over your metering base. The basic revenue protection principles incorporated are:

  • Discovering and knowing the location of a meter
  • Knowing the operational status of a meter
  • Detecting faults
  • Detecting tampering
  • Callout management
  • Real-time control with work order management and GIS tracking of:
    • site audits
    • installations
    • replacements
    • repairs
    • inspections
  • Electronic monitoring of meters
